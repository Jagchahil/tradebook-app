# 37: Competitor and Review Audit

> What the competition has, what their users complain about, and exactly what we change so Lekhio is not missing anything and gives customers what they want. Researched June 2026 against FreeAgent, QuickBooks, Xero, Coconut, untied, ANNA, GoSimpleTax.

---

## The competitors, in one line each

- **FreeAgent.** Loved, intuitive, MTD ready, free if you bank with NatWest, RBS, Mettle, else £19/mo. Aimed at established freelancers.
- **QuickBooks.** £10 to £16/mo, strong tax tools, GPS mileage, but support is widely criticised and prices keep rising.
- **Xero.** £16 to £59/mo, powerful, but more than a sole trader needs and pricier.
- **Coconut.** Mobile first, HMRC recognised MTD, Open Banking feed, invoicing, smart categorisation. Closest to us.
- **untied.** Personal tax app, auto mileage, bank feed, files directly to HMRC. Closest to us.
- **ANNA.** Free business account plus invoicing, expenses, MTD.

## What their users complain about, and how we avoid each

From App Store, Trustpilot, Capterra, G2 reviews of the above.

1. **Support is slow and you cannot reach a human.** The number one complaint, especially QuickBooks. **Our answer:** a real person replies on the same WhatsApp. Lead with it on the site.
2. **Prices keep rising, and there are receipt limits and hidden fees.** **Our answer:** one flat £29, unlimited everything, no tiers. Say it plainly.
3. **It talks to you like an accountant. Not intuitive.** **Our answer:** plain English, no jargon, it lives in WhatsApp. Already our voice.
4. **Slow and clunky.** **Our answer:** keep the product fast. A product quality bar, not a message.
5. **Hard to cancel.** **Our answer:** cancel any time in a tap, and say so. Add it to the site.
6. **Too much clutter, more than a sole trader needs.** **Our answer:** we do the few things trades need, brilliantly, not fifty features they will never touch.
7. **Receipt scanning gets it wrong.** **Our answer:** you review and approve every entry, and fixing one is a tap. Already true, show it.
