# 01: Vision

## The Problem

There are approximately 750,000 self-employed construction workers in the UK, and over 1.3 million self-employed tradespeople overall. electricians, plumbers, plasterers, joiners, painters, roofers. They are skilled, busy, and almost universally terrible at admin. not because they're incapable, but because every tool built for small business finance was built by people who sit at desks.

The average tradesperson:
- Works 8 to 10 hour days on-site
- Handles 20 to 50 receipts and expenses per week
- Has no idea what they owe HMRC until an accountant tells them
- Pays £500 to 2,000/year for an accountant to file a return that takes 30 minutes
- Has no time, no laptop on site, and no patience for accounting software

The existing tools (FreeAgent £19/mo, QuickBooks £22 to 39/mo, Xero £15 to 30/mo) are desktop-first, form-heavy, and designed for people who understand double-entry bookkeeping. None of them are built for a plasterer who finishes a job at 5pm and needs to log £83 of materials he bought at Screwfix this morning.

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax Self Assessment (MTD ITSA) makes this urgent:
- **April 2026**: Sole traders earning >£50k must use MTD-compliant software (mandatory NOW)
- **April 2027**: Threshold drops to >£30k
- **April 2028**: Threshold drops to >£20k

By 2028, virtually every self-employed tradesperson in the UK is legally required to use compliant software. HMRC is creating the market. We are building the right product for it.

## The User

**Primary user:** Self-employed UK tradesperson, sole trader.
- Earnings: £30k to £150k/year
- Works alone or with 1 to 2 employees or subbies
- On-site most of the day
- iPhone or Android, always on WhatsApp
- Paying Checkatrade £60 to 120/mo for leads they may or may not get
- Currently tracking expenses in a notes app, a spreadsheet, or not at all

**They are not unintelligent. They are time-poor and on-site.**

They do not want to learn software. They want things handled. They already use WhatsApp for everything. quoting, ordering materials, managing clients. That is their operating system.
