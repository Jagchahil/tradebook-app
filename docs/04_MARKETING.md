## Principle

Organic first. Prove what converts. Then scale hard with paid.

Running aggressive paid spend before the product is live and before we know what messaging converts is money on fire. The sequence below is aggressive but intelligent. it builds to a high-velocity paid engine, not a guessing game.

## Pricing

**Single tier: £12.99/month.** No annual lock-in at launch.

**Why £12.99:**
- Checkatrade charges £60 to 120/mo with no guaranteed return. Tradespeople are pre-conditioned to spend here.
- FreeAgent charges £19/mo for a desktop app nobody on site uses. We undercut it AND live where the work happens.
- £12.99 is less than a Nando's, and a fraction of an hour of a tradesperson's labour. Easy to justify.
- Competitive to acquire at this price point: LTV is £12.99 x average retention. Target 18+ months = £234 LTV.

**Phase 2 pricing:** £49/mo (back-office + marketing creatives engine)
**Phase 3 pricing:** £69/mo (everything including Connect jobs network)

## GTM Phases

### Phase 0. Waitlist (Now, Zero Spend)

Goal: 500 waitlist signups before launch.

**Landing page:** One-page site. Hero: "Text it. Your Lekhio handles it." Subhead: "The WhatsApp bookkeeping assistant built for UK tradespeople. MTD sorted. No forms. No apps. Just WhatsApp." CTA: "Join the waitlist → get 60 days free when we launch."

**Organic channels (start immediately):**
- TikTok @lekhio.app: "POV: you're a plasterer and your admin is sorted" content. Day-in-the-life format. Pain point content.
- Instagram Reels: same content repurposed
- Facebook Groups: UK trades groups (there are 50+ with 10k to 200k members each). Not spam. Genuine participation + occasional posts.
- Reddit: r/UKPersonalFinance, r/selfemployed: genuine helpful posts about MTD, link to Lekhio waitlist

**No paid spend in Phase 0.**

---

### Phase 1. Test Spend (Launch Month, £500 to 1,000/mo)

Goal: Find the creative and audience combination that converts at < £15 CAC.

Run 5 to 8 ad variants across Meta. Test:
- Hook A: Pain point ("Do you know what you owe HMRC?")
- Hook B: Demo ("Watch this plasterer log a receipt in 3 seconds")
- Hook C: Price anchor ("Stop paying £100/mo for Checkatrade leads that don't show up")
- Hook D: MTD urgency ("HMRC is making it mandatory. Are you ready?")

**Audiences to test:**
- UK, 25 to 55, interests: construction, electrical work, plumbing, self-employment
- Lookalike from waitlist email list (once 500+)
- Retargeting: website visitors who didn't sign up

**Measure:** Cost per signup (waitlist), cost per activation (WhatsApp first message), cost per conversion (paid subscriber).

---

### Phase 2. Scale (Once CAC is Known)

Once we know one creative format + audience converts at < £15 CAC, scale the budget hard.

**The creative engine (Claude + Higgsfield):**

Each week, the system generates:
- 3 short-form videos (15 to 30 seconds) via Higgsfield: realistic tradesperson-on-site scenarios
- 5 Meta carousel ads (5-slide format) via Claude + image generation
- 3 TikTok-format videos (hook + demo + CTA)
- Copy variants for each

**Video concept library:**
- "Plasterer texts a receipt from the van": Higgsfield generates realistic footage
- "Before/after: Monday morning with spreadsheets vs Monday morning with Lekhio"
- "How I stopped losing £200/month": testimonial style, AI-generated
- "HMRC is watching": MTD urgency angle
- "Your accountant charges £1,500. Lekhio costs £12.99.": price comparison

**Approval gate:** Jag reviews and approves all creatives before any spend goes live. Non-negotiable. This is an irreversible financial action.

**Budget Phase 2:** £2,000 to 5,000/mo, scaling with ROAS.

## Content Strategy (Organic)

### TikTok / Instagram Reels
Post 3 to 5x per week. Formats:
1. **Demo content:** Screen recording of WhatsApp conversation + receipt parse. Real, unpolished, authentic.
2. **Pain point content:** "POV: it's 9pm and you're trying to work out your quarterly tax". relatable, emotional.
3. **Educational:** "What is MTD and does it affect you?". genuinely helpful, positions Lekhio as the solution.
4. **Social proof (once live):** Real users showing their WhatsApp thread. Permission obtained.

### Facebook Groups Strategy
Target groups: "UK Tradesmen", "UK Builders & Contractors", "Self Employed UK", "Electricians UK Forum", "UK Plumbers Network". collectively 500k+ members.

Approach: Participate genuinely for 2 weeks (helpful comments about MTD, tax, tools). Then post: "I built something for this. happy to share if useful." Never post a direct sales link cold.

## The Lekhio Brand Voice

- Straight-talking. No jargon.
- Like a smart mate who's sorted, not a software company.
- British. Not American. Say "sorted" not "solved."
- Confident without being cocky.
- Knows the trades. References real situations: Screwfix, B&Q, scaffolding, CIS, subbies.

**Never say:** "leverage", "synergy", "onboard", "seamless", "robust", "solution"
**Always say:** "sorted", "handled", "text it", "done", "it's in your Lekhio", "it's logged"
