## Description (both stores)

Lekhio is the back office for anyone self employed in the UK. Snap a receipt, leave a voice note, or type "spent £40 on diesel" on WhatsApp, and it is logged, sorted and ready for tax. No spreadsheets, no shoebox of receipts, no evenings lost to paperwork.

TEXT IT, IT IS LOGGED
Photograph a receipt and Lekhio reads the total, the shop and the date, and files it under the right category. Say it out loud in a voice note if your hands are full. Or just type it. Everything lands in the app for you to check and confirm.

BUILT FOR THE TRADES
Electricians, plumbers, builders, plasterers, roofers, joiners, decorators, gas engineers, scaffolders, landscapers, and every other sole trader. CIS done properly: text "Dave paid £400, £80 CIS deducted" and Lekhio keeps the gross, the deduction and your refund building up. Mileage in a text at the HMRC rate. Invoices written and sent from a message.

TAX PREPARED FOR YOU. YOU APPROVE EVERYTHING.
Your income and expenses add up as you go. Making Tax Digital asks for four short updates a year, and Lekhio prepares each one for you to check. Nothing is ever sent to HMRC without your say so. You stay in charge, always.

ASK ANYTHING
"How much have I spent this month?" "Can I claim my work boots?" "When is my tax due?" Ask in plain English on WhatsApp and get a straight answer from your own figures.

WHAT IT COSTS
£12.99 a month or £129 a year, everything in. Your first 30 days are free, no card needed.

THE SMALL PRINT, HONESTLY
Lekhio is an independent UK company. We are not HMRC and HMRC does not endorse us. Lekhio prepares your figures; you approve them; you stay responsible for your tax. General guidance, not regulated tax advice. Figures use the England, Wales and Northern Ireland income tax bands.
