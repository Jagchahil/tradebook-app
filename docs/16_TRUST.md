# 16: Trust

> How Lekhio earns trust with people who are right to be cautious. We touch money, tax, and WhatsApp, the three things scammers abuse most. Trust is not a section on the website, it is the product. This doc is the audit and the playbook. Research gathered June 2026.

---

## The problem, plainly

We are asking a self employed person to send photos of their receipts and talk about their money to a number on WhatsApp, and we mention tax. To a cautious person that can pattern match to three fears:

1. **"Is this an HMRC scam?"** HMRC received over 135,000 scam reports in a single year. The classic scam is a tax themed message, a sense of urgency, and a request for bank details or a login. We must look like the exact opposite of that.
2. **"Are you stealing or selling my data?"** Financial and personal data. People assume the worst unless told otherwise, clearly and early.
3. **"Are you invading my privacy through WhatsApp?"** People worry we can read their other chats or watch their phone. We cannot, and we must say so.

If we do not answer these out loud, a chunk of people will never start, no matter how good the product is.

---

## What erodes trust (avoid all of these)

- Tax themed urgency. Anything that reads like "act now or HMRC will fine you."
- Asking for sensitive details in chat: bank details, card numbers, passwords, login codes, National Insurance number.
- Us messaging first, out of the blue. That is what scammers do.
- Vague ownership. Not saying who we are, no company, no real address, no named people.
- Implying we are HMRC, or that HMRC endorses us.
- Typos and sloppy design. In finance, a single typo reads as fraud.
- Hidden pricing, surprise charges, hard to cancel. Erodes trust even when the product is honest.

---

## What builds trust (do all of these)

- **You always start the conversation.** Lekhio only ever replies to a message you send first. We spell this out.
- **We never ask for the dangerous stuff.** No bank details, no passwords, no login codes, ever, by anyone claiming to be us.
- **You approve everything.** We prepare, you confirm. Nothing reaches HMRC without you. HMRC keeps you legally responsible. Submission only ever goes through an HMRC recognised route.
- **Your data is yours.** Never sold, never shared beyond the named suppliers that run the service. Export or delete it any time.
- **Plain security facts.** Encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you can see your records. We never log your message content to outside services.
- **A real company and real people.** Lekhio Ltd, UK based, registered with the ICO for data protection, working under UK GDPR. A human replies on the same WhatsApp.
- **Clean, calm, professional design.** No urgency, no shouting, no errors.
- **A clear "it is really us vs a scam" guide**, so people can tell Lekhio apart from a fraudster pretending to be Lekhio or HMRC.

---

## The "we will / we will never" device

This is the single strongest trust asset. Put it on the website and in the app, in plain words.

**Lekhio will**
- Only ever reply to messages you send us first.
- Show you every entry and wait for you to approve it.
- Prepare your tax figures and let you check them before anything is sent.
- Let you export or delete your data whenever you want.
- Reply as a real person when you ask for help.

**Lekhio will never**
- Text or call you out of the blue claiming you owe tax.
- Ask for your bank details, card number, passwords, or login codes.
- Send anything to HMRC without your say so.
- Sell your data or share it with anyone outside the suppliers that run the service.
- Pretend to be HMRC. We are not HMRC and HMRC does not endorse us.

---

## How we compare to a scam (use this framing)

A real HMRC scam: contacts you first, creates panic, asks for bank details or a payment, uses links to harvest logins. Lekhio: you message first, no panic, never asks for bank details or passwords, never asks you to pay a surprise bill. Teaching people the difference makes us look trustworthy and helps them in general, which builds goodwill.

---

## Where this lives

### Website
- A dedicated "Built to be trusted" section with four pillars: your data is yours, you approve everything, we never chase you, a real UK company.
- The we will / we will never lists, side by side.
- A short line near the tax explainer: "Lekhio is not HMRC and is not endorsed by HMRC. We prepare, you approve."
- Prominent Privacy and Terms links, a Security summary, and the company name in the footer (already present).

### App
- A trust line on the welcome and phone screens. The phone screen already says "No spam. No sharing." Keep and reinforce.
- A "Privacy and security" row in Settings that explains, in plain words: encrypted, only you see it, we never sell it, you approve everything, how to spot a scam.
- Never request sensitive data in any flow. The only thing we ask for is the mobile number, and we say why.

## Build and launch checklist

- [ ] Register with the ICO and pay the data protection fee (doc 14). This lets us say "registered with the ICO" truthfully.
- [ ] Publish a plain English Privacy Policy and Terms, linked everywhere.
- [ ] Confirm the MTD submission route is through HMRC recognised software, and only claim what is true.
- [ ] Verify the WhatsApp sender is an official Meta verified business number, so our name and green tick show.
- [ ] Set up support@lekhio.app with a real person behind it.
- [ ] Never put the words "file your tax for you" anywhere. We prepare, you approve.
- [ ] Proofread every line. Typos cost trust.
